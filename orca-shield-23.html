The Shifting Shadows
Edgar notices shadows aren't behaving. He understands light travels in straight lines, but something is distorting them.

Observation: The grandfather clock shadow flickers and bends, as if the light is swerving around an invisible object.
"The straight path is broken. Something is making the light swerve. Is there a blank spot in the air itself?"

The Disappearing Object
Edgar's red rubber bone vanishes when placed in the oily tackle box compartment.

Observation: Light passes through the object without reflecting back from certain angles. Index of Refraction match?
"My bone is there, I can smell it! But my eyes tell me it's not. Is the box eating the light?"

The Muted Glow-Ball
The phosphorescent ball shines dimly only near the 'Mysterious Blank' in the Den.

Observation: A dull flicker replaces the steady green luminescence. A 'hungry darkness' sips the light away.
"Is it stealing the light, or is the air itself absorbing the glow?"
